Your Real Estate, Powered by Skif-AluSteel. Manufacturing and installation of premium roofing and steel solutions. Quality. Durability. Satisfaction.

  1. 1.

    Skif Alu-Steel provides expert roof installation for both new construction and renovation projects. Specializing in premium stone-coated tiles and durable aluminum roofing sheets, our skilled technicians ensure precise, high-quality installation. We deliver comprehensive roofing solutions, safeguarding your investment with lasting protection and enhancing the aesthetic appeal of your property.

    Roof Installation

  2. 2.

    We provide precision installation of robust wood or steel rafters for residential, commercial, and industrial structures, ensuring optimal roof support and load distribution. Our specialized services extend to the meticulous removal and replacement of compromised rafters, including comprehensive structural assessments and code-compliant installation. Additionally, we offer expert repair and maintenance, reinforcing and restoring damaged rafters to guarantee lasting structural integrity.

    Raftering

  3. 3.

    Skif Alu-Steel delivers comprehensive roof solutions, from targeted renovations to complete replacements. We install premium stone-coated tiles, long span aluminum sheets, cladding, and diverse roofing systems, tailored to your budget and preferences. Our services restore, enhance, and extend roof lifespan, addressing existing issues while boosting performance, aesthetics, and energy efficiency. Protect your investment and elevate your property with our expert roofing services.

    Renovations

  4. 4.

    Address roof wear and tear promptly with Skif Alu-Steel's expert repair services. Our skilled technicians efficiently handle all roof damage, from minor leaks to extensive storm damage. We restore your roof to optimal condition, ensuring lasting protection and preventing costly future repairs. Trust us to safeguard your property with timely and reliable roof restoration.

    Roof Repair

  5. 5.

    We specialize in providing tailored, robust roofing solutions for diverse commercial properties, from office buildings to expansive industrial facilities. Leveraging years of industry expertise, we deliver unparalleled quality and durable protection. Our comprehensive services ensure lasting performance and safeguard your investment, tailored to meet the unique demands of your commercial roofing needs.

    Commercial Roofing

  6. 6.

    Skif Alu-Steel delivers customized, high-quality residential roofing, tailored to meet each homeowner's unique needs. We provide dependable, aesthetically pleasing roofs, safeguarding your home and enhancing its value. Secure your investment with our expert consultation and comprehensive residential roofing services. Contact us today for a complimentary assessment and personalized solutions.

    Residential Roofing

  7. 7.

    We provide precision installation for a comprehensive range of doors, including entry, interior, exterior, sliding, French, and patio doors. Our skilled technicians ensure seamless installation of all door hardware, guaranteeing optimal operation and enhanced security. We deliver tailored door solutions, ensuring both aesthetic appeal and lasting functionality for every project.

    Doors

  8. 8.

    We deliver precision installation of a comprehensive range of window styles, including single-hung, double-hung, casement, awning, sliding, picture, bay, and bow. Our meticulous process guarantees optimal fit, insulation, and seamless functionality. We also specialize in the efficient removal and replacement of outdated windows, offering custom sizing and disposal services to enhance your property's energy efficiency and aesthetic.

    Windows

  9. 9.

    Skif Alu-Steel offers expert consultation and design assistance for all your roofing, window, rafter, and door needs. Our team provides detailed assessments and tailored recommendations, guiding you through material selection, style choices, and installation methods. We ensure optimal solutions that align with your budget, preferences, and project requirements, guaranteeing lasting quality and aesthetic appeal.
